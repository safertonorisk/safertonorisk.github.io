About Me

Kristofer R. Sano

Innovator, inventor, and researcher driven by curiosity of the natural sciences and their practical applications. With a strong interest in nuclear science, radiation protection, and robotics , I have acquired relevant skills through diverse training programs, competitions, and academic courses. My current work focuses on instrumentation, dispersion-modelling, and problem-solving to promote safety, sustainability, environment protection.

Guided by a vision that science, technology, and engineering should shape mankind for good, I strive to develop solutions through research that have a meaningful impact on society.
