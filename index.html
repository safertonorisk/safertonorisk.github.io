Hi! I am Kristofer R. Sano

I am a researcher, innovator, and inventor from General Santos City, Philippines.
My research focus is on nuclear science, green energy materials and instrumentation. See my CV

#NuclearEnergy

Harnessing the power of the atom is as monumental as the discovery of fire. In the right hands, it can benefit humanity by providing solutions in healthcare, industry, and power generation. Read More at IAEA.

Instrumentation and Robotics

Remote monitoring of hazards and the use of robots reduce the risk to human health when dangerous situations arise. The challenge is making these devices cost-effective and efficient in their tasks. Watch Our Prev. Project.

Energy Materials

Environmental problems arise with existing power sources which are also strained by the growing demands of the global economy. New energy materials help offset, and in the future, dethrone the lead of harmful sources of energy. Read my Internship Adviser's Blog on Energy Materials.

Award-Winning Research

My hard work, with help from my colleagues and supervisor's, have garnered awards in prestigious competitions, colloquiums, and conferences. Beyond trophies and medals, it is my hope that these science and technology breakthroughs improve society.
